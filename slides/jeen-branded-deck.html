Slide 1 · Customer Context & Problem

Albion Mutual — a mid-size UK motor & home insurer

Who is the customer

  • UK general insurer, ~400k policies across Motor, Home, Travel & Liability
  • FCA-regulated; claims handled by a contact-centre plus a team of adjusters
  • First Notice of Loss (FNOL) arrives by web form, email and phone

The problem they brought to us

  • Every new claim is read and triaged by hand before it reaches the right team
  • High-value, fraud-suspect and vulnerable-customer cases are easy to miss in the queue
  • Result: backlog, slow first response, inconsistent decisions, regulatory exposure

Slide 2 · Why This Use Case

Triage is the highest-leverage place to put an agent

Current pain points

  • Manual classification & routing of every FNOL
  • No consistent, auditable fraud-risk scoring at intake
  • Vulnerable customers identified late, if at all
  • Slow acknowledgement erodes customer trust

Expected business value

  • Straight-through processing of clean, low-value claims
  • Adjusters spend time on the claims that need judgement
  • Consistent fraud & vulnerability flags from day one, fully audited
  • Faster first response, fewer SLA breaches
Low-risk to pilot — humans stay in the loop on anything sensitive

Why prioritise it: it touches a high-volume daily process, delivers measurable wins — speed, consistency, demonstrable Consumer-Duty compliance — within one quarter, and keeps a human accountable for every sensitive decision.

Slide 5 · Governance & Fit

From a pilot to production-grade, governed AI

Why this is the Jeen story

  • One flow orchestrates data + model + human oversight — not a chatbot bolted on the side
  • The LLM is scoped to a decision; actions and approvals are governed
  • Postgres gives a defensible audit trail for every claim and decision
  • Human-in-the-loop is a routing rule, not an afterthought

Where it goes next

  • Policy-system & fraud-database lookups as agent tools
  • RAG over policy wordings for coverage checks
  • Confidence thresholds & model fallback for resilience
  • Dashboards on triage accuracy, STP rate, SLA & vulnerable-customer outcomes

Deliverables: exported n8n workflow (JSON) · full-workflow screenshot · successful-run screenshots · sample dataset · this deck · plus a live hosted demo reviewers can try.

Appendix · What we built & delivered

Built hands-on, on our own infrastructure

How it was built & hosted

  • Built on a self-hosted, production n8n instance we already run — not a trial or n8n Cloud
  • Three workflows: the FNOL agent (form + human-in-the-loop), a triage API (webhook), and a hosted portal serving the UI
  • Exposed over the public internet via our Cloudflare tunnel — reviewers get a live, clickable URL
  • Stack: n8n · OpenAI GPT-4o-mini (structured output) · PostgreSQL (audit record) · Cloudflare
  • Triage logic validated against the live model across 6 scenarios — every routing decision correct
Live demo: a reviewer-testable portal URL is included with this submission

Assignment requirements — all met

  • LLM-powered decision step — structured triage (type, severity, fraud score, vulnerability, routing)
  • Action / integration — Postgres write + email acknowledgement (not just chat)
