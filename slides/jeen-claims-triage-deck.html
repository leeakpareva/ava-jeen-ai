Slide 1 · Customer Context & Problem

Albion Mutual — a mid-size UK motor & home insurer

Who is the customer

  • UK general insurer, ~400k policies across Motor, Home, Travel and Liability
  • FCA-regulated; claims handled by a contact-centre + a team of adjusters
  • First Notice of Loss (FNOL) arrives by web form, email and phone

The problem

  • Every new claim is read and triaged manually before it reaches the right team
  • High-value, fraud-suspect and vulnerable-customer cases are easy to miss in the queue
  • Backlog → slow first response, inconsistent decisions, regulatory exposure

Slide 2 · Why This Use Case

Triage is the highest-leverage place to put an agent

Current pain points

  • Manual classification & routing of every FNOL
  • No consistent, auditable fraud-risk scoring at intake
  • Vulnerable customers identified late, if at all
  • Slow acknowledgement hurts customer trust

Expected business value

  • Straight-through processing of clean, low-value claims
  • Adjusters spend time on the claims that need judgement
  • Consistent fraud & vulnerability flags on day one — audit trail in the system of record
  • Faster first response, fewer SLA breaches

Why prioritise this: it is low-risk to pilot (humans stay in the loop on anything sensitive), it touches a high-volume daily process, and it produces measurable wins — speed, consistency, and demonstrable Consumer-Duty compliance — within one quarter.

Slide 4 · Live Demo & Results

Four claims, four correct outcomes

What I run live

  • Low-value motor scrape → auto-settled, instant acknowledgement
  • £46k kitchen fire → held for adjuster approval
  • Recently-incepted, round-number theft, "settle this week" → flagged SIU-fraud, human review
  • Bereaved 78-year-old → vulnerability flag, routed to adjuster with care note

What you see

  • Structured triage JSON from the LLM (type, severity, fraud score + reasons, vulnerability)
  • Row appears in the Postgres claims table — full audit trail
  • Sensitive claim pauses on an in-product adjuster review form
  • Claimant receives a branded acknowledgement email with a claim reference

Every decision is logged and reversible. The agent accelerates the routine and surfaces the risky — it does not replace the adjuster.

From a pilot to production-grade, governed AI

Why this is the Jeen story
