The problem

Every claim is triaged by hand. That's slow, inconsistent, and risky.

🔻 Current pain points

  • Manual classification & routing of every First Notice of Loss
  • No consistent, auditable fraud-risk scoring at intake
  • Vulnerable customers identified late, if at all
  • Slow first response erodes trust and breaches SLAs

✦ What the agent delivers

  • Straight-through processing of clean, low-value claims
  • Adjusters spend time only on claims that need judgement
  • Consistent fraud & vulnerability flags from day one — fully audited
  • A defensible record of every decision, human or automated

How it works

One workflow orchestrating data, a model, and human oversight.

4 · Route

Human approval
Governed by design. The LLM is scoped to a decision — it never settles or rejects a claim on its own. High-value (≥£10k), high-fraud-risk (≥60) or vulnerable-customer claims are always held for a human adjuster to approve or reject. Every claim and every decision is logged, giving a defensible audit trail. Stack: n8n (orchestration) · OpenAI GPT-4o-mini (triage) · PostgreSQL (record) · Cloudflare (hosting).
